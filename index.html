Roadmap

Eight steps from revenue to global off-grid communities.

The roadmap: how every project and service contributes to one long-term build path.

01
Build revenue-generating businesses.

Launch IronClaw Security and other apps that create the capital base needed to fund everything else.

02
Form The Enlightenism Organization foundation.

Set up the non-profit structure that will help people relocate into off-grid communities in the best countries.

03
Route profits into direct support.

Use business profits to donate into the foundation so more families can be helped over time.

04
Reinvest into land development.

Acquire and build off-grid, farm-oriented communities so there are actual places available for people to live.

05
Expand into the strongest countries.

Grow the model into the best countries around the world so members can choose where they want to live.

06
Share real-world knowledge.

Educate members on health, geopolitics, and how the world is really structured so they can protect and improve their lives.

07
Establish member protections.

When the member base is strong enough, file for a registered belief system to help protect members from future government mandates and overreach using religious exemptions.

08
Keep building the legacy.

Continue expanding, refining, and funding the ecosystem so it can help as many families as possible for as long as possible.
