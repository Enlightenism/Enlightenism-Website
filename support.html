Help & Support

Helping families move from fragile situations into off-grid, farm-oriented communities.

This page explains the mission, how profits flow into charity, how membership works, and how donors can support different tiers so we can help more families leave unstable living situations.

Mission: give people a real way out.

The Enlightenism Organization exists to help people and families who do not like the living situation, or life they are in and want to move toward off-grid, farm-oriented communities that are healthier and more resilient. We use profits from our programs and businesses to donate into the non-profit foundation, which then supports relocation, land development, and long-term community building for families that need it.

Membership — free, skill-based, and mission-driven.

Becoming a member costs nothing. We just want to understand who you are, what you can do, and how you might fit into the communities and projects we are building, now or later.

Join as a member.

Use this form to tell us your full name, how to reach you, and what your profession or special skills are. This helps us match people with the right projects and communities.

Support tiers — Seed, Builder, Visionary.

Donor support helps us move faster: buying land, building off-grid communities, and covering relocation costs for families. Different tiers will receive different levels of rewards and access in the future as we build this with you.

Choose a donation tier.

These are the tiers. Seed donors will receive gifts from our companies, Builder donors get gifts and project access investment, and Visionary donors will receive gifts, project investment, and the opportunity to help shape entire community builds. With a heavier return based on donation amount.

Seed

Seed donors help plant the first stages of each project and community. Even small contributions make it possible to move one more family, buy one more piece of land, or build one more system.

Donate as Seed
Builder

Builder donors help us construct and scale the off-grid communities themselves — infrastructure, farming builds, and shared systems that people can actually live inside for the long term.

Donate as Builder
Visionary

Visionary donors help fund entire community builds and relocation waves. Over time, this tier will be tied into deeper strategic planning, special access, and long-range rewards for helping many families.
