Program development

Live security and upcoming programs built to help people directly.

These programs are being built to actually change how people live: protection while using AI, real language learning, lower-cost animal fostering, friend finding for awake people, and daily brain training.

IronClaw Security

AI brain with layered protection against prompt injection.

IronClaw Security is an AI security system designed to protect users while they are using AI coding tools and other powerful models. It runs a multi-layer Python and FastAPI stack that watches for prompt injection and hostile patterns, so people can experiment and build without getting quietly exploited by malicious prompts.

Live now Available today

Langy Language Learning

Full language understanding, not just memorized phrases.

Langy is being built to teach people the actual structure of a language: grammar, patterns, context, and how native speakers really use it in everyday life. Instead of only teaching fixed phrases, Langy will help people reach a point where they can think and express themselves in the new language with confidence.

Upcoming Launch target: October 2026

Animal Fosterz

Sharing ad revenue to lower the cost of fostering animals.

Animal Fosterz is being built so foster parents share advertising profits from the app, which helps cover food and care costs. When fostering an animal is cheaper and supported, more people can say yes to fostering, which means more animals get safe homes instead of staying in cages.

Upcoming Launch target: December 2026

Awake Together

Friend finding and connection for people who see the real world.

Awake Together is being built to help people who understand the real world we live in find each other, become friends, set up events, and even find partners. The goal is to replace isolation with real community, where people can share plans, information, and support in person, not just online.

Upcoming Launch target: March 2027

Brain Games

AI-generated brain games at different difficulty levels.

Brain Games is being built to generate daily mental challenges at different levels, so people can keep their minds sharp over time. Instead of scrolling endlessly, people can run focused brain training sessions that feel like a game but still protect and grow their thinking power.

Upcoming Launch target: April 2027
